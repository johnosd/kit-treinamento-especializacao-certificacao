# Bibliografia - MBA em Engenharia de Dados, IA e Arquitetura de Plataformas

## 1. Núcleo Fundamental (Leitura Obrigatória)

Esses livros formam a base conceitual do programa e sustentam os conhecimentos em arquitetura, sistemas distribuídos, engenharia de software e plataformas de IA.

- *Designing Data-Intensive Applications* - Martin Kleppmann
- *Fundamentals of Software Architecture* - Mark Richards, Neal Ford
- *Clean Architecture: A Craftsman's Guide to Software Structure and Design* - Robert C. Martin
- *Designing Machine Learning Systems* - Chip Huyen
- *Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras & TensorFlow* - Aurélien Géron
- *Site Reliability Engineering: How Google Runs Production Systems* - Betsy Beyer et al.
- *Accelerate: The Science of Lean Software and DevOps* - Nicole Forsgren, Jez Humble, Gene Kim
- *Staff Engineer: Leadership Beyond the Management Track* - Will Larson
- *The Trusted Advisor* - David H. Maister, Charles H. Green, Robert M. Galford
- *System Design Interview - An Insider's Guide (Vol. 1)* - Alex Xu
